Welcome to The Forest School Hackathon!

Join us for an exhilarating event where creativity meets technology. Over the course of this Hackathon, you'll have the opportunity to push the boundaries of innovation, collaborate with fellow students, and develop solutions to real-world problems. Whether you have not got an idea of how to code or are a seasoned pro, this event is the perfect platform to showcase your skills, learn new ones, and be a part of a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts.

Get ready to embark on a journey of coding, creativity, and community. We can't wait to see what you'll create!
