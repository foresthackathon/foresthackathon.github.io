THE FOREST SCHOOL HACKATHON: IGNITING INNOVATION

Unlock your coding destiny.

Karim and Keon are proud to present its inaugural Hackathon, a thrilling coding event designed to foster creativity, collaboration, and real-world problem-solving among our brightest minds. Over the course of 6 months, students will dive deep into the digital realm, turning abstract ideas into tangible solutions that can lead to technological breakthroughs.
Who Can Participate?
Open exclusively to GCSE students of The Forest School, this event calls upon coders, designers, and innovators to come together in teams and harness the power of technology.
What's the Goal?
The objective is simple yet challenging: identify a problem, conceptualize a solution, and develop a prototype all within the duration of the Hackathon. Projects can range from software applications to data analysis frameworks, from AI-driven tools to digital games â€” anything that shows the promise of computer science.
Why Join?
Enhance your coding prowess, learn new frameworks, and polish your problem-solving skills.
Collaborate with peers, learn from mentors, and engage with experts in various tech fields.
Use this platform to bring your most innovative ideas to life and make an impact.
Outstanding projects will be recognized and awarded, with opportunities for further development.
How Does It Work?
1) Register as a group or sign up individually.
2) Attend the opening ceremony to learn about the themes and guidelines.
3) Spend the next 6 months creating your project, with resources and mentors at hand.
4) Follow the submission guidelines to enter your project for judging.
5) Showcase your work to judges and peers.
6) Celebrate success, innovation, and hard work as we wrap up with awards and recognitions.
Join us for an event that celebrates the spirit of discovery and the joy of creating. The Forest School Hackathon isn't just a competition; it's a stepping stone to the future. Be a part of it, and let's code a new tomorrow!
