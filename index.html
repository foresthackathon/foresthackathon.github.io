Welcome to The Forest School Hackathon!

Unlock your coding destiny

Karim and Keon are proud to present its inaugural Hackathon, a thrilling coding event designed to foster creativity, collaboration, and real-world problem-solving among our brightest minds. Over the course of 6 months, students will dive deep into the digital realm, turning abstract ideas into tangible solutions that can lead to technological breakthroughs.


Who can participate?


Open exclusively to GCSE students of The Forest School, this event calls upon coders, designers, and innovators to come together in teams and harness the power of technology.


What is the goal of the Hackathon?


The objective is simple yet challenging: identify a problem, conceptualize a solution, and develop a prototype all within the duration of the Hackathon. Projects can range from software applications to data analysis frameworks, from AI-driven tools to digital games — anything that shows the promise of computer science.


Why join?

  • Skill Development: Enhance your coding prowess, learn new frameworks, and polish your problem-solving skills.
  • Networking: Collaborate with peers, learn from mentors, and engage with experts in various tech fields.
  • Innovation: Use this platform to bring your most innovative ideas to life and make an impact.
  • Recognition and Prizes: Outstanding projects will be recognized and awarded, with opportunities for further development.
